INKY Tombola

This feature is a community rewards system funded by transaction taxes. Its rules are entirely governed by the INKY Council to ensure fairness and transparency.

Rules: Determined by the INKY Council vote for fairness.

Funding: Powered by a small tax on every $INKY transaction.

Prize: The winner receives the capped Prize Pool amount, and the surplus is sent to burn.

Frequency: Automatic draws on the NXRA chain.
